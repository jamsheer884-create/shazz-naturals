Our Story

Born from Kerala's
Natural Abundance

Shazz Natural's was born from a deep love for Kerala's extraordinary botanical richness. Surrounded by lush green forests, Ayurvedic heritage, and ancient beauty secrets passed down through generations, we began crafting small-batch natural beauty products that truly work.

Every product is handmade using ingredients you can actually recognise — no complex chemicals, no synthetic fragrances, no hidden nasties. Just pure botanical goodness from God's Own Country, lovingly crafted for your skin and hair.

From the healing touch of Kerala neem to the brightening power of papaya, from beetroot's natural lip colour to the time-tested magic of henna — we bottle the best of Kerala's nature for you.
